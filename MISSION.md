# Mission: Electrician Learning Workspace

## Why

This workspace is for learning electrician-related knowledge through short ChatGPT teach-lite sessions and Codex-generated HTML lessons. The exact real-world target should be refined before the first full lesson.

## Success looks like

- The learner can study small, practical electrician topics through mobile-friendly summaries.
- The learner can review reusable cheat sheets from `reference/`.
- Codex can generate self-contained HTML lessons in `lessons/`.
- Progress and next actions are preserved across sessions.

## Constraints

- Keep answers and mobile lesson notes short.
- Prefer one small lesson per session.
- Do not commit paid PDFs, work documents, private source materials, or personal information.
- Store only summaries, citations, and source descriptions in Git.

## Out of scope

- Uploading or committing copyrighted paid source material.
- Storing private workplace data or personal documents.
- Large course generation before the mission and learner level are clarified.
