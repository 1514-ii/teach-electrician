# Learning Record Format

Learning records live in `learning-records/` and use sequential filenames:

- `0001-dash-case-title.md`
- `0002-dash-case-title.md`

Template:

```md
# {Short title}

{1-3 sentences explaining what was learned or established, and why it matters for future lessons.}
```

Use a learning record when:

- the learner demonstrates real understanding
- the learner states relevant prior knowledge
- a misconception is corrected
- the mission changes after confirmation

Do not use learning records for:

- material merely covered
- generic session logs
- copied source notes
